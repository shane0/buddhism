four common

  1. precious human life - gratitude for being born human with cognizance
  2. impermanence - come to terms with the impermanence of your body and all objects
  3. karma - everyone's conduct has causes and effects
  4. suffering - do not add to the naturally occurring suffering

  2. purification - clean your body, speech & mind
